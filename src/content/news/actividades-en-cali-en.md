# JamCam 2025 Cali scouts enjoy their activities, discover some of them!

JamCam 2025 Cali participants enjoy activities inside and outside Comfandi Pance, while learning about the invited countries through the Global Village stands.

JamCam 2025 Cali activities are divided into four subcamps, which rotate according to the event program and camp day. These include the Cali tour, the visit to the Global Village, extreme games within Comfandi and more.

During their tour of the Global Village, boys, girls and young people can learn about each participating country, enjoy cultural presentations and be part of unique workshops at each contingent stand. Some of these activities include Colombian coffee tasting, collecting ducks to earn a badge in Puerto Rico, and learning about traditions from Peru, among other experiences.

## How have JamCam 2025 Cali participants felt about the activities?

JamCam 2025 Cali participants have expressed their enthusiasm during all activities. The boys and girls at the event comment that it is very exciting to be with people from other countries and learn about their cultures. In addition, for many it has been a new experience to be able to try sweets from Mexico, Chile, Argentina, Puerto Rico and Colombia all in one place.

## What else can be found in the JamCam 2025 Cali activity program?

In the JamCam 2025 Cali activity programming, exclusive nighttime events have been prepared for each subcamp. Children, youth and young people have enjoyed integrations on stage with musical activities and cultural presentations for them. In addition, through surveys, participants recognize Patrol Leaders for their work.
