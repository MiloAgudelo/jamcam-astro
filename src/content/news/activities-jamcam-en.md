JamCam 2025 will offer a wide variety of activities designed to challenge, inspire, and entertain all participants.

## Main Activities

Our program includes water sports, climbing, cultural activities, educational workshops, and much more. Each day will be filled with new experiences and opportunities to learn.

## Cultural Exchange

One of the most special features of JamCam is the opportunity to meet scouts from different countries and cultures. You can share traditions, learn languages, and make friends from all over the Americas.

## Scout Challenges

Get ready to test your scout skills in various challenges that will test your teamwork, leadership, and spirit of adventure.

Don't miss this incredible experience!
