We are pleased to welcome you to JamCam 2025! This historic event will bring together scouts from across the Americas in Cali, Colombia, from December 26, 2025 to January 2, 2026.

## A Unique Event

JamCam 2025 will be an unforgettable experience where young people from across the region will share adventures, learn new skills, and forge lifelong friendships.

## Get Ready for Adventure

We will be sharing more information about activities, programs, and registration details in the coming weeks. Stay tuned for our updates!
