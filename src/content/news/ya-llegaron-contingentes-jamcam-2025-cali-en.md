# The contingents have arrived at JamCam 2025 in Cali! Learn the details

An adventure that brings together more than 2,000 young people from different parts of America. During this week, they will have fun, exchange, and connect with each other.

Day one of JamCam 2025 Cali has begun, participating delegations have started setting up their campsite for these 7 days. During the event, participating children and young people will be able to enjoy activities, tours, services, and cultural exchanges among participating countries.

## What activities and tours are prepared for Camporee and Jamboree participants in Cali?

From December 27 to January 2, Camporee and Jamboree participants will be able to explore new spaces getting to know the city of Cali through tours of its most representative places, where they will be able to interact with the Cali Fair, participate in salsa workshops and community services.

During the camp, the arrival of the new year 2026 will take place, on December 31 the host delegation Colombia has prepared a cultural celebration and shows by guest artists to make it a special night in union with all JamCam 2025 Cali participants.
The boys and girls are looking forward to the event, with excitement they await the camp inauguration and are ready to begin with all the activities prepared for them, in addition, this space will be an opportunity to meet other cultures and people from different countries.

## What do children and young people feel on their first day of camp?

JamCam 2025 Cali participants arrive with great energy at Comfandi Pance, they are ready for the adventure planned for these days. With euphoria they express their enthusiasm to meet other delegations and their culture, especially because of the bond that unites them in shared values as scouts.

## What are the expectations of JamCam 2025 Cali participants?

During JamCam 2025 Cali, participants eagerly look forward to getting to know the cultural diversity of participating countries, exchange with them, and forge new friendships.
